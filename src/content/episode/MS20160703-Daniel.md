The concluding sermon in the Daniel 9 series focuses on the phrase "he shall make a strong covenant" in verse 27 and the identity of the one who brings sacrifice and offering to an end. Riddlebarger argues that this refers to Christ's atoning death — not to a future Antichrist — and shows how this reading coheres with the prophecy's earlier references to the cutting off of the Anointed One. The sermon draws together the pastoral significance of Daniel's vision for Christian hope.
