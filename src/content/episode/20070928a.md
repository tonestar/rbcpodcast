The opening lecture of Riddlebarger's "Eschatology and the Antichrist" series at Christ Reformed Church. He surveys the biblical data on the antichrist figure across the Old and New Testaments, examining the Johannine letters, Daniel, and 2 Thessalonians. Riddlebarger argues for a robustly exegetical doctrine of antichrist that avoids both liberal dismissiveness and the sensationalist speculation common in popular prophecy culture.
