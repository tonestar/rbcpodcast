The central future expectation of the entire New Testament is the bodily, personal, visible return of Jesus Christ. This lecture surveys the key texts — the Olivet Discourse, Paul's letters to the Thessalonians, and Revelation 19–20 — to establish what the New Testament actually teaches about the parousia. Riddlebarger addresses common misconceptions about a secret rapture and a two-stage return, arguing that the biblical data points consistently to a single, glorious appearing of Christ at the end of the age.
