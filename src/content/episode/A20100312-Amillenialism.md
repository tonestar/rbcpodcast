Continuing the examination of the blessed hope, Riddlebarger explores the New Testament's teaching on the resurrection of the dead and final judgment in connection with Christ's return. He addresses key passages in 1 Thessalonians 4–5, 1 Corinthians 15, and Revelation 20–22, showing how the hope of bodily resurrection fits within the amillennial framework and gives the church a robust, this-worldly eschatological horizon.
