Continuing the introduction to biblical prophecy, Riddlebarger examines the Old Testament foundation of New Testament eschatology. He shows how the prophets developed themes of exile and return, judgment and restoration, that find their ultimate fulfilment not in a restored national Israel but in Christ and his church. Special attention is given to the hermeneutical question of whether and how New Testament authors interpret Old Testament prophecy — and what that means for our own reading of unfulfilled prophecy.
