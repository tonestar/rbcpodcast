The opening lecture of a thirteen-part series on biblical prophecy. Riddlebarger frames the question: given that so much prophecy has already been fulfilled in Christ, what actually remains to be fulfilled? He introduces the hermeneutical principles necessary for interpreting prophetic Scripture and surveys the landscape of competing eschatological systems — dispensationalism, historic premillennialism, postmillennialism, and amillennialism — before laying the groundwork for the lectures to come.
