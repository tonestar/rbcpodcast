The concluding lecture of the Eschatology and the Antichrist series addresses a significant challenge from within the Reformed tradition: full preterism. Riddlebarger engages the preterist claim that all New Testament prophecy was fulfilled by AD 70 and explains why this position, while raising legitimate historical questions, ultimately fails exegetically and is incompatible with orthodox Christianity. He defends the partial-preterist position as the Reformed via media.
