The concluding lecture of the series synthesises all that has come before and sketches what the amillennial reading of Scripture expects as the age draws to a close. Riddlebarger addresses the general resurrection, the final judgment, the new heavens and new earth, and the eternal state — showing how the biblical story that began in Eden moves to its glorious consummation in a renewed creation. He closes with a pastoral reflection on why a robust, Reformed eschatology produces hope, comfort, and patient endurance rather than speculation and anxiety.
