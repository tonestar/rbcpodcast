Riddlebarger demonstrates how the eschatological framework of amillennialism flows directly from covenant theology. The covenant of grace, running from Genesis to Revelation, means there is one people of God, one hope, and one fulfillment — in Jesus Christ. This lecture shows that eschatology is not merely one strand of theology but is woven into the entire biblical-covenantal storyline.
