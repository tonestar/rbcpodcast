Riddlebarger addresses the charge that amillennialism teaches "replacement theology" — the idea that the church has simply replaced Israel, rendering God's Old Testament promises void. He argues this framing is a caricature: rather than replacement, the New Testament teaches fulfilment. Christ is the true Israel; the church, Jew and Gentile united, is the Israel of God (Gal. 6:16). God has not abandoned his promises — he has kept every single one of them in Christ.
