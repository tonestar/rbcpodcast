Who or what is the Antichrist? Riddlebarger works through the key biblical texts — the Johannine epistles, 2 Thessalonians 2, and Revelation — to reconstruct the New Testament's composite portrait of the "man of lawlessness" and the spirit of antichrist. He evaluates the historic identification with the papacy found in Reformation-era commentaries as well as modern speculative identifications, arguing that the biblical picture describes both a recurring historical pattern of apostasy and a final, personal manifestation of lawlessness before the return of Christ.
