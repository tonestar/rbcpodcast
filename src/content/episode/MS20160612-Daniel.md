Preaching from Daniel 9:1–19, Riddlebarger examines Daniel's great penitential prayer — one of the most moving passages in all of Scripture. He shows how Daniel, meditating on Jeremiah's prophecy of seventy years, turns to God in confession on behalf of his people. The prayer is a model of corporate repentance grounded in the character of God: merciful, forgiving, and faithful to his covenant promises even when his people have been faithless.
