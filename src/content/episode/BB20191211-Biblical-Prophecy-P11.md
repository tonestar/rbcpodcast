An extended lecture — nearly double the usual length — examining the signs that the New Testament says will herald the return of Christ. Riddlebarger works systematically through the Olivet Discourse (Matthew 24–25), identifying which predictions were fulfilled in the events of A.D. 70 and which point to signs accompanying Christ's final appearing. He discusses the worldwide preaching of the gospel, the great tribulation, cosmic disturbances, and the sign of the Son of Man, and addresses the preterist, futurist, and idealist interpretive traditions.
