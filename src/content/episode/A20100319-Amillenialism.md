A direct engagement with the doctrine of the rapture as taught in dispensationalist circles. Riddlebarger examines the key proof texts — 1 Thessalonians 4:13–18 and Matthew 24:40–41 — and argues they do not support a secret pre-tribulation rapture but describe the single, public, glorious return of Christ at the end of history, at which all believers (living and dead) will be gathered to meet the Lord. The rapture and the second coming are one and the same event.
