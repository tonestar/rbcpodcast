An exegetical examination of Matthew 24:1–14, the opening of the Olivet Discourse. Riddlebarger identifies the signs Jesus gives — wars, famines, earthquakes, false christs, persecution — and argues they describe conditions characteristic of the entire interadvental age, not a compressed tribulation period immediately before Christ's return. The gospel being proclaimed to all nations is the decisive sign before the end.
