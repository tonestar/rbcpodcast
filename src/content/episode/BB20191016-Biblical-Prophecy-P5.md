Exploring one of the most important questions in Reformed eschatology: what is the relationship between the biblical covenants and last things? Riddlebarger shows how the covenant of grace, progressively revealed through Adam, Noah, Abraham, Moses, and David, reaches its telos in the new covenant inaugurated by Christ. He argues that a covenantal hermeneutic — rather than a dispensational one — provides the most coherent account of how the Old Testament promises are fulfilled in Christ and his church.
