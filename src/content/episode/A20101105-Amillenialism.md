Perhaps the most theologically dense lecture in the series. Riddlebarger works through Paul's argument in Romans 9–11 — covering divine election, the remnant of Israel, the hardening of unbelieving Israel, the ingrafting of the Gentiles, and whether "all Israel will be saved." He defends a reading in which "all Israel" refers to the full number of elect Jews and Gentiles together, rather than a future mass conversion of ethnic Israel.
