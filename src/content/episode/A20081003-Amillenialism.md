Continuing the previous session on fulfilled prophecy, Riddlebarger presses further into the Old Testament prophetic texts that were foretold and are now accomplished in Christ. Building on Part 1's survey of typology and promise-fulfilment, this lecture works through specific passages that dispensationalism assigns to a future earthly kingdom, demonstrating how the New Testament authors understood them as already fulfilled in Christ's first advent and ongoing reign.
