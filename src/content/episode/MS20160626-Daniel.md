A careful sermon on Daniel 9:20–27, one of the most debated passages in the whole Bible. Riddlebarger works through the structure of the seventy-weeks prophecy — the decree, the seven weeks, the sixty-two weeks, and the final week — and shows how its six stated goals (Dan. 9:24) point to the coming of the Messiah. He engages the major interpretive options and argues for a Christological fulfilment in contrast to the dispensationalist "gap theory."
