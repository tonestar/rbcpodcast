Does the Reformed view teach that the church has simply replaced Israel, discarding God's covenant people? Riddlebarger directly addresses the accusation of "replacement theology" — a charge frequently levelled at amillennialism. He argues that the proper category is not replacement but fulfilment: the church, composed of believing Jews and Gentiles, is the continuation and expansion of the one people of God, and Old Testament promises are fulfilled in Christ and his body rather than set aside or deferred.
